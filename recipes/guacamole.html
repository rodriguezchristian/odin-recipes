Guacamole Recipe

Description

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make.

Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasonings
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Mash the avocado
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients
  3. Chill in the fridge.
